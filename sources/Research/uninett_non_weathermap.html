The research network - always at the forefront

Research Network a high capacity network that links the internal IT networks of educational and forskningsinstistusjoner throughout Norway, from Ny-Ålesund in the north to Kristiansand in the south. Moreover, the research network connections to other networks, both commercial networks in Norway and international research networks, the latter through the Nordic association of research networks, NORDUnet .

The research network is developed and run by UNINETT and should be far ahead of what is available from commercial suppliers in terms of bandwidth, availability and functionality. Security is at a high level without unnecessary restrictions on openness and functionality, and each customer has the opportunity to assess these considerations against each other.

Hybrid network with high capacity and reliability

The core of the research network is a hybrid network that consists of a flexible optical transport networks where dedicated channels of 1, 2.5 and 10 Gigabit per second can easily be set up. This gives UNINETT's customers the opportunity to set up høgkapasitetsforbindelser of particularly demanding applications, eg. in connection with:

  • supercomputing
  • distributed storage of large amounts of data
  • high-resolution real-time video
  • eVLBI / radio astronomy

The optical core network also provides the basis for the main lines of 10 Gbit / s in the shared IP network - a network that is connected in a ring structure, that is, with more lines to the same point. This provides increased reliability and uptime, and reduce significantly the chance of losing the network connection if the single connection fails.

Robustness and reliability

UNINETT has a joint operations center for network and services are available for customers' IT operations personnel 24 / 7 Network structure is built to provide optimum redundancy at low cost by a customer's primary connection in some cases a person's reservevei in the event of mains failure. Not only line guides, but also the choice of end point of the lines helps to ensure this: hardware failure or a local power failure affecting a network node will not be able to take down both routes to a customer that is associated with a ring structure.

Functionality

UNINETT assigns customers sufficiently large address blocks that all machines, whether the individual end-user can be assigned a globally unique IP address. This allows each user to a full-fledged two-way player in
network rather than a passive information consumers with a one-way "keyhole" to the rest of the Internet. New network technology into early use in conjunction with network research among customers, and transferred to the production network as soon as the experience of this application shows that it is technically feasible without harm to existing traffic. Examples of such technology that is available in the research network is IPv6 and Multicast.

UNINETT offers a variety of network services and support that customers can optionally use. Among these are common solutions developed in cooperation with the customers that make it possible for the university and høggskolesektoren in Norway to take out significant gains both in terms of reduced costs and customized solutions out of reach for a single customer.
